About the shelter "Cozy House"

Welcome to Cozy House, a leading animal shelter dedicated to providing safe haven for abandoned and homeless pets. For over 20 years, we have been committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue animals from dangerous situations, provide them with medical care, proper nutrition, and emotional support while they await adoption into loving homes.

What We Do

  • Rescue abandoned and abused animals
  • Provide veterinary care and rehabilitation
  • Offer socialization and behavioral training
  • Match pets with suitable forever homes
  • Educate the community about animal welfare
